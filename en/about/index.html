Business Activities

The Institute for Information Accessibility engages in the following activities:

  1. Accessibility of Digital Content
    Research, development, production, and consulting related to digital content accessibility. In particular, the Institute provides certification of EPUB publications based on EPUB Accessibility, as defined by JIS and the W3C Recommendation.

  2. Copyright Management Technologies (e.g., LCP)
    Research, development, and consulting on copyright management technologies such as LCP.

  3. Electronic Publications (e.g., EPUB)
    Production, distribution, certification, and infrastructure development for electronic publications.

  4. Computer-Based Testing (CBT) and Personalized Learning Support (PNP)
    System research, development, and consulting related to CBT and PNP.

  5. Accessibility Improvement for Educational Content
    Improving the accessibility of digital textbooks, learning materials, and related educational resources.

  6. Presentation Optimization for Individual Needs
    Research and implementation of presentation optimization technologies tailored to individual user preferences \ including read-aloud, font customization, and layout adjustment.

  7. Related Activities
    Other activities related or incidental to the above.
